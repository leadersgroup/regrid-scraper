# 50Deeds.com Sales Commission Plan

## Service Overview
- **Service:** Trust deed preparation and recording for out-of-state properties
- **Price:** $299 per deed
- **Target Client:** Trust and estate planning attorneys
- **Average Established Client Volume:** 10 orders/month (30 orders/quarter)

---

## Commission Structure (Quarterly)

### Tier 1: Foundation (Base Rate)
**Quarterly Revenue:** $0 - $25,000
- **Commission Rate:** 5%
- **Example Earnings:** $1,250 on $25,000 revenue
- **Equivalent:** ~2.8 established clients OR 84 total orders

### Tier 2: Growth
**Quarterly Revenue:** $25,001 - $50,000
- **Commission Rate:** 6%
- **Example Earnings:** $1,500 (on incremental $25,000) + $1,250 = $2,750 total
- **Equivalent:** 3-5 established clients OR 84-167 total orders

### Tier 3: Momentum
**Quarterly Revenue:** $50,001 - $100,000
- **Commission Rate:** 7%
- **Example Earnings:** $3,500 (on incremental $50,000) + $2,750 = $6,250 total
- **Equivalent:** 6-11 established clients OR 168-334 total orders

### Tier 4: Excellence
**Quarterly Revenue:** $100,001 - $200,000
- **Commission Rate:** 8%
- **Example Earnings:** $8,000 (on incremental $100,000) + $6,250 = $14,250 total
- **Equivalent:** 12-22 established clients OR 335-669 total orders

### Tier 5: Elite
**Quarterly Revenue:** $200,001+
- **Commission Rate:** 10%
- **Example Earnings:** 10% on all revenue above $200,000 + $14,250 base
- **Equivalent:** 23+ established clients OR 670+ total orders

---

## Bonus Accelerators

### New Client Acquisition Bonus
- **First order from new client:** $250 one-time bonus

### Quarterly Performance Bonuses
- **Hit Tier 3 or above:** +$500 quarterly bonus
- **Hit Tier 5:** +$2,000 quarterly bonus

### Annual Accelerator
- **If annual revenue exceeds $500,000:** Additional 2% commission on total annual revenue (paid at year-end)

## Commission Examples

- **Revenue:** $18,000 (2 established clients, 60 orders)
- **Base Commission:** $900 (5%)
- **New Client Bonuses:** $500 (2 clients × $250)
- **Total Earnings:** $1,400

### Scenario 2: Growing Rep (Quarter 2)
- **Revenue:** $45,000 (5 established clients, 150 orders)
- **Base Commission:** $2,450
  - Tier 1: $25,000 × 5% = $1,250
  - Tier 2: $20,000 × 6% = $1,200
- **New Client Bonuses:** $750 (3 new clients × $250)
- **Total Earnings:** $3,200

### Scenario 3: Top Performer (Quarter 3)
- **Revenue:** $120,000 (13 established clients, 401 orders)
- **Base Commission:** $7,850
  - Tier 1: $25,000 × 5% = $1,250
  - Tier 2: $25,000 × 6% = $1,500
  - Tier 3: $50,000 × 7% = $3,500
  - Tier 4: $20,000 × 8% = $1,600
- **New Client Bonuses:** $1,000 (4 new clients × $250)
- **Tier 4 Performance Bonus:** $500
- **Total Earnings:** $9,350

### Scenario 4: Elite Performer (Quarter 4)
- **Revenue:** $250,000 (28 established clients, 836 orders)
- **Base Commission:** $19,250
  - Tier 1-4: $14,250 (cumulative from tiers 1-4)
  - Tier 5: $50,000 × 10% = $5,000
- **Performance Bonuses:** $2,500
- **Total Quarterly Earnings:** $21,750
- **Annual Bonus (if applicable):** Additional 2% on annual revenue

---

## Commission Payment Terms

1. **Payment Timing:** Paid within 15 days after quarter end
2. **Qualified Revenue:** Only revenue from completed and paid orders counts toward commission
3. **Refunds/Chargebacks:** Deducted from next quarter's commission calculation
4. **New Client Definition:** Any attorney or law firm not previously serviced by 50Deeds.com
5. **Established Client Definition:** Client placing 10+ orders per month for 2 consecutive months

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Sales reps will be evaluated on:
- **Total Quarterly Revenue**
- **Number of New Clients Acquired**
- **Client Retention Rate**
- **Average Orders per Client**
- **Client Satisfaction Score** (optional metric for bonus consideration)

---

## Notes

- Commission tiers are **progressive** (you earn higher rates on incremental revenue)
- All bonuses are paid in addition to base commission
- Plan subject to annual review and adjustment
- Early termination: Commissions paid only on closed/paid deals as of termination date
