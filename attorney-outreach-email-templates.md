## CAMPAIGN STRATEGY OVERVIEW

### Email Sequence Framework
- **Week 1**: Initial Contact (Template 1A or 1B)
- **Week 2**: Follow-Up if no response (Template 2A)
- **Week 3**: Value Proposition Focus (Template 3A or 3B)
- **Week 4**: Educational Content (Template 4A)
- **Month 2-3**: Quarterly nurture emails (Templates 4B, 5A)
- **Month 4+**: Re-engagement if dormant (Templates 5B, 5C)

### Audience Segmentation Recommendations
- **Trust Attorneys**: Emphasize probate avoidance, trust administration efficiency
- **Estate Planning Attorneys**: Focus on comprehensive client service, document coordination
- **High-Volume Practices**: Highlight scalability, bulk pricing, turnaround speed
- **Solo Practitioners**: Emphasize ease of use, no overhead, professional support

### A/B Testing Framework

**Test Variables (One at a Time):**

1. **Subject Lines**
   - Curiosity vs. Benefit-driven
   - Short (< 50 chars) vs. Long (50-70 chars)
   - Question vs. Statement
   - Personalized vs. Generic

2. **Email Length**
   - Short form (150-200 words)
   - Medium form (250-350 words)
   - Long form (400-500 words)

3. **Opening Hook**
   - Problem-focused
   - Benefit-focused
   - Story/anecdote
   - Direct value proposition

4. **Call-to-Action**
   - Calendar link vs. Reply request
   - Single CTA vs. Multiple options
   - Soft ask vs. Direct ask
   - Time-limited vs. Open-ended

5. **Tone**
   - Formal/professional
   - Conversational/collegial
   - Educational/thought leadership
   - Urgent/time-sensitive

6. **Social Proof Elements**
   - With testimonials vs. Without
   - With case studies vs. Without
   - With statistics vs. Without
   - Named references vs. Anonymous

**Testing Protocol:**
- Minimum sample size: 100 recipients per variant
- Test one variable at a time
- Run test for 7-14 days before analyzing
- Track: Open rate, click-through rate, reply rate, conversion rate
- Implement winner, then test next variable

- Maintain professional tone consistent with attorney communications
